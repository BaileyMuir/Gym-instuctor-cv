you will find a list of definitions below for the terminology used.

  • tempo - in this case relates to the time you take to complete the motion e.g. bicep curl i gave 2 secons to bring it towards your chest and 4 to lower it under controll to complete one full movement.
  • reps - used for saying the number of times that a particular exercise should be repeated in a sports workout. Rep is short for repetition.
  • sets - a group of repeated movements performed as part of a period of physical exercise e.g. how we take a break after we complete a set amount of reps would equal one set of that exercise.
  • time - this relates to the amount of time to complete the exercise in, this doesnt mean do it once but as many times as you can in the case of the plank you are required to hold the position for a minute.
  • rest - the time taken between each set to recupparate.
  • db - Dumbbell
  • BB - Barbell
  • EZ - EZ bar
  • hypertrophy - referse to an increase in size, structure, or function in the case used here we refere to the increase in size of the muscle fibres.
