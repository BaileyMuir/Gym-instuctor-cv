Summary

The program below is designed to target the whole body incorporating the aspect of hypertrophy training(muscle growth) using bodyweight, free weights and machine-based exercises, each of which has its own benefits, but briefly, the machine chest press allows additional support and will allow you to develop the necessary muscle control needed to perform a free weight bench press but that being said free weight emphasises the use of muscle control and balance.


As stated before, I'm using the hypertrophy section of the strength-endurance continuum, which places it in between both an image will be provided. For this reason, the exercises are all between 8-12 reps and 2-3 sets. If you require additional help, click the help option in the footer below.
